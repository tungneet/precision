Our Story

Born from the conviction that India's sporting potential remains largely untapped, Precision Play emerged as a bridge between cutting-edge technology and athletic excellence.

Our founder's journey began with a bold decision to forgo traditional career paths and pursue Olympic qualification firsthand. This immersive experience revealed critical gaps in India's sports ecosystem - not just in funding, but in the fundamental approach to athletic development and performance optimization.

Through partnerships with premier organizations like the Rowing Federation of India and Athletics Federation of India, we've witnessed the transformative power of integrating science with sport. The success of Indian hockey, attributed partly to sports science innovation, serves as our north star.

Elite Partnerships

Collaborating with India's premier sports federations including RFI and AFI to implement next-generation training methodologies and performance analytics across multiple disciplines.

Evidence-Based Approach

Every solution we develop is grounded in rigorous sports science research, validated through real-world testing with elite athletes and coaching staff.

Systemic Transformation

We're not just building tools - we're reshaping how India thinks about sports careers, making athletic excellence a respected and viable professional path.

Technology Solutions

Our AI-powered platform integrates seamlessly with existing training infrastructure, providing coaches and athletes with unprecedented insights into performance optimization.

Intelligent Performance Analytics

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze biomechanical data, training loads, and competition results to identify optimization opportunities and predict performance outcomes.

Predictive Injury Prevention

AI models that monitor athlete wellness, movement patterns, and training stress to predict injury risk and recommend preventive interventions before problems occur.

Personalized Training Systems

Dynamic training programs that adapt in real-time based on performance data, recovery metrics, and competition schedules to maximize athletic development.

Real-time Coaching Insights

Live feedback systems that provide coaches with instant performance analysis during training sessions, enabling immediate technique corrections and tactical adjustments.

Talent Discovery Platform

Comprehensive assessment tools that identify promising young athletes through performance testing, movement analysis, and predictive modeling across multiple sports.

Strategic Competition Analysis

Deep analysis of opponent patterns, competition trends, and tactical opportunities to help athletes and coaches develop winning strategies for major competitions.
