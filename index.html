About Precision Play

Precision Play was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between India's sporting potential and its Olympic performance. Our team of engineers, sports scientists, and technology experts recognized that India's sporting challenges extend far beyond funding, they require a fundamental shift in how we approach athletic development.

We identified the core issue: while recreation in sports is widely accepted, building a career in competitive sports faces systemic barriers. Our solution focuses on creating the technological and scientific infrastructure that world-class sporting nations rely on, but adapted specifically for the Indian context.

Born from the conviction that India deserves its place among the world's sporting superpowers, Precision Play represents a new generation of sports technology companies. We're not just building tools, we're architecting the future of Indian athletics, one innovation at a time.
