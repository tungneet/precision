About Our Journey

Right after graduating, I made a pivotal decision...not to follow the conventional path of joining an organization, despite securing a place through university placement. Instead, I chose to follow my heart over my brain, dedicating the next few years to qualifying for the Olympics.

Along the way, I realized that the real deficiencies in India's sports environment go beyond just funding. The deeper problem is mindset: "Recreation is accepted. A career in sports is not." If we truly want to see India succeed at the Olympics, we need more engineers, doctors, and scientists...people who will build the science behind sports.

The moment the Olympics 2024 Closing Ceremony ended, we made a decision...to start Precision Play. India is my country, and its challenges are my challenges. We're taking ownership of the problem and building the solution.
