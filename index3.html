About Precision Play

Right after graduating, our founder made a pivotal decision - not to follow the conventional path of joining an organization, despite securing a place through university placement. Instead, choosing to follow the heart over the brain, dedicating years to qualifying for the Olympics.

The journey was about experiencing it firsthand, mapping out the route to the top, and understanding what it truly takes to reach the highest level in sports. The road was full of challenges, but for every obstacle, we found solutions.

Along the way, we realized that the real deficiencies in India's sports environment go beyond just funding. While financial constraints remain a fundamental challenge, the deeper problem is mindset: "Recreation is accepted. A career in sports is not."

Our Partnerships

We've worked with prestigious organizations including the Rowing Federation of India (RFI) and Athletics Federation of India (AFI), bringing cutting-edge technology to Indian sports.

Sports Science Focus

Inspired by Piyush K. Dubey's insights on how sports science contributed to the Indian men's hockey team's success, we're embedding science into existing training systems.

Our Technology

Today, it's time to embed sports science into our existing training and performance systems through cutting-edge AI technology.

Performance Analytics

Advanced AI algorithms analyze athlete performance data to identify patterns, predict outcomes, and suggest optimization strategies for training and competition.

Injury Prevention

Predictive models that assess injury risk based on training load, biomechanical data, and historical patterns to keep athletes healthy and competing.

Personalized Training

AI-driven training programs that adapt to individual athlete needs, optimizing workouts based on performance data, recovery metrics, and competition schedules.

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous monitoring systems that provide instant feedback during training sessions, helping athletes and coaches make immediate adjustments.

Talent Identification

AI systems that help identify promising young athletes by analyzing performance metrics and predicting future potential across various sports disciplines.

Competition Strategy

Data-driven strategic insights that help athletes and coaches prepare for competitions by analyzing opponent patterns and optimizing game plans.
