Our Mission

About Us

Right after graduating, I made a pivotal decision—not to follow the conventional path of joining an organization, despite securing a place through university placement. Instead, I chose to follow my heart over my brain, dedicating the next few years to qualifying for the Olympics.

I wanted to experience the journey firsthand, map out the route to the top, and understand what it truly takes to reach the highest level in sports. The road was full of challenges, but for every obstacle, we found solutions.

Identifying Core Issues

The real deficiencies in India's sports environment go beyond just funding. The deeper problem is mindset: "Recreation is accepted. A career in sports is not."

Taking Ownership

If we have the right to ask why we were not truly successful at the 2024 Olympics, we should also take proportional responsibility for it. India is my country, and its challenges are my challenges.

Building Solutions

We need more engineers, doctors, and scientists—people who will build the science behind sports, not just as a job, but as a responsibility.

Our Mission

Making India win 100 gold medals at the next five Olympics (2028, 2030, 2032, 2034, 2036)

The moment the Olympics 2024 Closing Ceremony ended, we made a decision—to start a sports science company: Precision Play. We are building a technology layer for the Indian sports ecosystem, leveraging AI.

Piyush K. Dubey, former head coach of the Indian men's hockey team, credited sports science for the team's success over the past decade. Today, it is time to embed sports science into our existing training and performance systems.

Sports Science Integration

Embedding advanced sports science into existing training and performance systems across Indian sports infrastructure.

AI-Powered Analytics

Leveraging artificial intelligence to analyze performance data, optimize training programs, and predict athletic potential.

Technology Layer

Building comprehensive technology solutions that connect athletes, coaches, and sports organizations for maximum efficiency.
